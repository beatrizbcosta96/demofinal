Hi! My name is Beatriz, and I am originally from Lisbon, Portugal. I am currently based in Australia on a Work & Holiday Visa, exploring new opportunities.

With a background in Biomedical Engineering, I decided to invest in my passion for Graphic Design and enrolled in a course at Lisbon School of Design – LSD (2021/2022), where I had the opportunity to learn about Logotype & Visual Identity, Packaging, Editorial, Advertising and Typography. 

Besides loving science and graphic design, I really enjoy taking pictures and editing them, and have taken several mini-courses in this field.
