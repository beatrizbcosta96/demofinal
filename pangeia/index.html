Pangeia

LISBON, 2021

Pangeia is a monthly magazine about the world, that intends to bring together the different parts of the world through the knowledge, reminding us of the time we were only one continent (Pangeia). The magazine is composed of three main sections: wildlife, eco-friendly tourism and gastronomy. It also contains a section with curiosities about a country or region.
